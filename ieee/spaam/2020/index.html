SPAAM

About The Event Free Event

Student Professional Awareness Activities Month or SPAAM, is a one-month long event whose main focus is to develop professional awareness among students.The event is organized such that students planning for any kind of career path can benefit from it. We have speakers from diverse backgrounds who will cover sessions regarding higher studies, entrepreneurship, and a job oriented career.
Considering the current situation, we will be going completely online and thus extending our resources to a global audience! . After each session, the participants will be given an opportunity to interact with the speakers to clarify their doubts. So stay tuned and let’s get constructive and productive this quarantine!
