Professional Integrity: Student to Professional Talk

Treading down the path from a student to a professional is never an easy one. It is important to practice ethics in a work environment that is constantly influenced by the realities of globalization, financial motivation, and information overload and to balance professional integrity against corporate loyalty and other personal interests. Look out for this session to take your first steps to be an ideal professional.

  • Date: 21th September 2021
  • Time: 6:00 PM, IST
  • Speaker(s): Mr Eugene Kingsley
  • Eligibility: Open to all

EUGENE KINGSLEY

Mr Eugene Kingsley is a Technical Solutions Specialist at Kissflow Inc. He has years of experience working in the IT industry, in various sectors mainly, data science and big data management. He is a volunteer and a true professional at heart.
