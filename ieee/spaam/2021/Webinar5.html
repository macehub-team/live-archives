Know about robotics

Robotics has the potential to positively transform lives and work practices, raise efficiency and safety levels and provide enhanced levels of service. Even more, it is set to become the driving technology underpinning a whole new generation of autonomous devices. Recent unveiling of the Tesla Bot is a sign that robotics is here to stay. The speaker will help get you started in the field of Robotics.

  • Date: 23rd September 2021
  • Time: 7:30 PM, IST
  • Speaker(s): Prof. Shankar J
  • Eligibility: Open to all

SHANKAR J

 Mr. Shankar J

Mr. Shankar J serves as an assistant professor at UKF College of Engineering and Technology and at IEEE Kerala Section, presently serving as the chairperson of IEEE Student Activities Committee. He also volunteers for IEEE Kerala Young Professionals as the Academia Working Group Coordinator. He has been honored many times for his leadership quality and dedication as a volunteer. His areas of interest include wireless communication, robotics, IoT and Brain-Computer Interface.
